Our Story

A little corner built for lingering

The Nook started as a dream: a place where the coffee is never rushed and every regular has a name. We roast in small batches, bake before dawn, and keep the playlist soft enough for conversation.

Whether you're here for a quick flat white or a lazy Sunday brunch with friends, we saved you the best seat by the window.

  • Single-origin & house blends
  • All-day brunch & pastries
  • Locally sourced ingredients
